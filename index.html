Committed to Your Safety

Your peace of mind is our priority. We've integrated safety features into every part of the Rhapid experience, from the moment you book a ride to the moment you arrive at your destination. We're dedicated to making every trip a safe and comfortable one.

Comprehensive Driver Screening

Every driver on the Rhapid platform must pass a multi-stage screening process, including a comprehensive background check and vehicle inspection, to ensure they meet our high standards.

Two-Way Ratings

Both riders and drivers rate each other after every trip. This system promotes a respectful and accountable community, and it allows us to take action on any feedback that violates our guidelines.

In-App Safety Tools

Share your trip status with trusted contacts, and access emergency assistance directly from the app. We put control at your fingertips so you can ride with confidence.
